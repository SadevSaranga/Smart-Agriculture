ðŸ§ª Quick Soil Test

Before setting up your Wet Zone, test your soil:

  1. Squeeze Test: Grab a handful of moist soil and squeeze. It should form a ball that crumbles easily when poked.
  2. Drainage Test: Water should absorb within 5-15 seconds, not pool on surface or drain instantly.
  3. Moisture Retention: Soil should stay moist (not soggy) for 24-48 hours after watering.
