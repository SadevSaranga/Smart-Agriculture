ðŸ§ª Quick Soil Test for Dry Zone

Before setting up your Dry Zone, verify soil drainage:

  1. Drainage Test: Pour water on soil - it should drain through within 30 seconds to 2 minutes.
  2. Squeeze Test: Moist soil should not form a ball and should crumble immediately when squeezed.
  3. Drying Time: Soil should be completely dry 1-3 inches deep within 3-5 days after watering.
